A free Minecraft utility client for anarchy servers

Ares is a free Minecraft utility client aimed at anarchy servers. Ares Client has many pvp features and exploits especially related to crystal pvp. It is maintained so that it has everything needed in a pvp client without the regular modules found in Impact or Future.


The client is currently being developed by me, Tigermouthbear, but has been developed in the past by Cookiedragon234 and Carbolemons.
