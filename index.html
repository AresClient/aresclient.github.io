A free Minecraft utility client for anarchy servers

Ares is a free Minecraft utility client aimed at anarchy servers. Ares Client has many pvp features and exploits especially related to crystal pvp. It has some of the best combat modules of any client.


The client is currently developed by me, Tigermouthbear. Credit to cookiedragon234 for a few of the modules tho
